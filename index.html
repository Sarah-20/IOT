About Us

We are Smartwatts

SmartWatts is a graduation project by students from the Department of Telecommunications and Electronics Engineering, aimed at developing an intelligent system to monitor and analyze energy consumption within factories using Internet of Things (IoT) technologies. The system relies on measuring real-time energy data through sensor units connected to a controller, and then the data is sent to a central server where it is displayed and analyzed on an interactive control interface (Dashboard). The system is not limited to data collection; it also includes an advanced security aspect to protect the data from tampering or cyberattacks. SmartWatts aims to enable factories to monitor their energy consumption in real time, improve efficiency, reduce losses, and contribute to cost reduction and higher sustainability
